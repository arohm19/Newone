Abdi Ali Bacalwaan:

fooraaw u jiilaal fidhidiisu badan tahay fasalkiyo daruurtuna fidha ay isku joojeen,
fadka miiran yeeshee fooshii aan de’inyo ma faraaci baa tahay firdhisaye jacaylkii sowdigaa ka fara baxay.


Sahro Axmed Jaamac:

Fooxlaha islaamaha faraqooda baadhee, fasaqaayo diintiyo war ma faaliyaa tahay?. Maxa falagan kugu diray,
warka faaladiisana, faahfaahinmaayee, fahanoo u jeedada "faraskaagii laga badi”

Abdi Ali Bacalwaan:

Goormaa fagaaraha furimaha colaadeed faraskaygii laga tagey maadigaan foyoobayn,
Adigaa fidmada fin jacayl ma guuree nabarkaagii fiix noqoy firdhisaya jacaylkii sawdigaan ka fara baxay.

Sahro Axmed Jaamac:

waan kaa fogeeyoo wax aanad filanayn warku sidii falaadhii wuu kaa fajiciyaa,
farta boogta aan daro aan fururayaa waxa dhacay faalada markaan nabar ku fadhiistay keligay faraskaagii laga badi.

Maxa falagan kugu diray, warka faaladiisana, faahfaahinmaayee, fahanoo u jeedada faraskaagii laga badi.

Wixii qeeldaaf ku jira dib ha loo hagaajiyo ama ha loo saxo.

Mahadsanidiin.?
